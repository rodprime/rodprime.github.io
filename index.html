Welcome to The Clear Side. Every week, I'll walk through the learning process here, so you can too.

Challenging concepts thrown about make it hard to navigate.

By [anni g] · First Dispatch

Half the words in the news — inflation, tariffs, the debt ceiling, quantitative easing — get used like everyone already understands them. Most people don't, and that's not their fault. These topics are usually explained by people who've already spent years studying them, in language built for other experts, not for someone trying to understand the news over breakfast.

This site is my attempt to close that gap, in both directions. I'm working through economics and government coursework this year, and instead of keeping my notes to myself, I'm turning what I learn into short, plain-language explainers — one topic a week, picked from whatever's actually in the news. If I can explain it clearly, that means I actually understood it. If you walk away understanding it too, even better.
