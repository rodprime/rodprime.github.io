The Manifesto

Born out of a summer afternoon with nothing to do, The Clear Side is more than a site, it's a belief. The political thinking sphere can be more accessible for people like you and me; we only need to be committed to baby steps of understanding. Most language is built for those with years of experience, but it doesn't have to be that way.

The Clear Side is my attempt to bridge the gap. I'm working through government and economics this school year, and I've decided to let you see my notes. I'll take the high-level ideas I learn and turn them into digestible overviews — once a week, from a topic that's probably in your news feed. If I can explain it lucidly, that means I get it. If you understand it, even better. Welcome to The Clear Side.
